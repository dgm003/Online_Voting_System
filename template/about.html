Welcome to the E-Voting System

Our online voting system is secure, reliable, and easy to use. Learn more about how we keep your votes safe and your identity secure.

Key Features

Our E-Voting platform offers the following features:

  • Secure online voting
  • Face authentication for voter identity verification
  • Two-step verification for additional security
  • User-friendly interface
  • Real-time voting results

How It Works

The voting process is straightforward:

  • Login with your registered credentials
  • Verify your identity using face recognition
  • Enter the system and choose your candidates
  • Confirm your vote and submit
  • Your vote is securely recorded and counted

Security Features

Your security is our priority. Our system includes:

  • Face recognition technology to prevent fraud
  • Two-step verification to ensure voter identity
  • End-to-end encryption of your vote data
  • Secure, tamper-proof database
